What kind of action did I take? What kind of action can you guys take?

We can do so many things with our great talents and capabilities. For me, I'm good at drawing and designing, so I can take action by making posters. These are two of my posters.

Here are some examples of "taking action" to get you started :

1. Make posters and spread the awareness by putting them up wherever you can.

2. Say NO! to plastic.

3. Do beach cleanups daily or whenever you can.

4. Spread awareness by communication.

5. Do a presentation to your class and family about microplastics.

6. Drink filtered tap water.
