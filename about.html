A poem that shares my interests and passions:


The Rise

By Jin Sagong

An invisible bubble

wraps tightly

around its victim,

the Earth

Determined

to stay

I wish

the once blue planet

could rise

rise from the ashes

The ashes from

the past

the present

the future

Free from

the flames

the toxic fumes

the fear of losing another soul

Determined

to make a difference

I cry out

to the world
