About HNPC

HNPC is a club founded by Jin Sagong with a group of ESS students, all willing to make our community and our planet a better place. We fundraise, donate, volunteer, beach cleanup, spread kindness, and raise awareness for people and animals in need.

HNPC club has donated to BC SPCA, Canadian Red Cross-3X Jasper Wildfires 2024, Holy Childhood Association, and ESS. We have decided to donate to BC Children’s hospital and The Doors Is Open with the fundraised money at the recent ESS Advent Market.

Support our community and donate today! To: jessieheo40@gmail.com!

50%-80% of the money raised goes to charity.
